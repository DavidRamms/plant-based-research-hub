While reminder interventions have been proposed as a potential solution, their effectiveness remains unclear. In this randomized clinical control trial, we found that the patient reminder program had no significant effect on adherence in postmenopausal women with osteoporosis.

Stumpf U, Kyvernitakis I, Horas K et al., 2025 · RCT · Bone Density & Fracture Risk
